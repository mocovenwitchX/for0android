Gnu Privacy Guard now provides encryption integrated into the Android experience:

* send encrypted files via the Contacts/People app  "Encrypt File To"
* encrypt files, media, and text, by sharing it to Gnu Privacy Guard
* view encrypted files by sharing with Gnu Privacy Guard
* import keys into your keyring in a few different ways:
  + click on a .pkr, .skr, .key file in your email, dropbox, SD card, etc.
  + click open a URL like: openpgp4fpr:9F0FE587374BBE81
  + scan a fingerprint QRCode
